Creative Coding Journey

March 10, 2024

My journey into creative coding began with curiosity—how can code become a tool for art? I started experimenting with generative visuals, interactive animations, and playful web experiments. Along the way, I discovered a vibrant community of artists and developers who share a passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the browser. Creative coding has taught me to embrace mistakes, iterate quickly, and always keep learning. It's a space where logic meets imagination, and I love sharing my discoveries through tutorials and open-source projects.
