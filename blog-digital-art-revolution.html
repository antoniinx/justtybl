Digital Art Revolution

March 5, 2024

The rise of AI and new digital tools is transforming the world of art. As a digital artist, I'm fascinated by how algorithms can inspire new forms of creativity. From neural networks that generate surreal images to code that creates endless patterns, the possibilities are limitless. But technology is just a tool—the real magic happens when artists use it to express their unique vision. In my work, I explore the intersection of code, color, and composition, always searching for new ways to surprise and delight viewers.
