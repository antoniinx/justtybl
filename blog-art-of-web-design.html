The Art of Web Design

March 15, 2024

Web design is more than just making things look pretty. It's about creating an experience that is both visually appealing and highly functional. In today's world, users expect websites to be fast, intuitive, and accessible on any device. My approach to web design combines bold color, playful layouts, and a focus on usability. I believe that every pixel should have a purpose, and every interaction should feel natural. Whether I'm designing a portfolio, a blog, or a landing page, I always start with the user in mind and iterate until the result feels just right.
